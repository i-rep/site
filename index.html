Empowering women and girls to fully enjoy their Human Rights

Working towards a society free from all forms of gender based violence and discrimination against women and girls

30% of girls do not live to their full potential

Violence against women and girls such as Female Genital Mutilation (FGM), rape, defilement, child marriage, sexual assault and domestic violence are globally recognized and condemned as breaching basic human rights. I_Rep Foundation was founded to conduct information dissemination and sensitization against all forms of violence against women and girls with an aim of creating awareness of the causes and consequences of violence and build capacity of partners and volunteers to prevent and respond to violence.

"Empower the girl, empower the community"

Our Mission

To be the influential voice to end all forms of gender based violence and abuse in order to achieve a safe society that recognizes, values and respects the rights and dignity of girls and women.

Our Vision

A society free from all forms of violence against women and girls.

Our Goals

Defending the rights of the vulnerable members of the community by strengthening the voice of women and the community to speak out against FGM, child marriages, rape among others

Why we do what we do

The vastness of our community and the cultural connotations makes it difficult to reach the victims in their rural villages in times of need. The Foundation employs a voluntary “trumpet call” to the community to engage fully in decision making and in the process of development to abandon these retrogressive cultural practices that serve to disenfranchise women from actively participating in community activities while at the same time achieving their life dreams

"I am responsible"

Most society disputes are because we are not all responsible. Taking into account that we are all part of the society, if we all change our way of thinking, being, acting and investing our money, our society model will change for good.

Personal Social Responsibility is the way we as individuals perform our daily life as members of the society –basing our decisions on a desire to minimize the negative impacts and maximize the positive impacts on the social, environmental and economic in the long run. This range of personal behavior, directly and indirectly shows the economic, legal, ethical, discretionary and environmental actions derived from the individual's role in the society, the environment and the world as a whole. Challenges occur when community members overlook the impact of their actions on others as well as themselves in the long run
